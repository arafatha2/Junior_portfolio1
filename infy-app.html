About Our Project

The Chill Capybaras is a mental wellness web app created by Aarya (Developer), Syeda (Developer), and Grace (Content Creator) from Thomas Edison CTE High School. Designed to help students manage stress through meditation exercises, mood tracking, and calming activities, our app brings the peaceful energy of capybaras to your mental health routine.

As part of the Generation Infy program, we wanted to create something that would make a real difference in people's lives. Mental health is particularly important for young people, and we aimed to build an accessible solution.
